Among Us was first released in June of 2018 to only Android and Ios, accumulating an average daily player base of only 30-50 players. The game also only had its one original map playable at the time.

By June 11th of 2020, two more maps, MIRA HQ and Polus, were released into the game.

These maps originally costed $4USD to play, then $2USD and eventually were made free to play.

It was only until July of 2020 where a twitch streamer named Sodapoppin started popularising the game via twitch. Soon after, many other big streamers such as xQc and Toast started popularising the game too, leading to a sudden massive surge of new players.

As the Covid Pandemic was at its peak during 2020, this contributed more to the game's popularity as it allowed players to socialise and have fun online with one another while social distancing in real life.

Due to all the growth in popularity, as of September of 2020, the game had more than 10 million owners on Steam and of Novemeber 2020, 500 million players worldwide.

Of course, Among Us won multiple awards due to its growth as a game and its popularity amongst players from all over the world. Some of the biggest awards presented to Among Us are such as Nickelodean Kid's Choice Awards with being the favourite video game of the year and Mobile Game Awards with being nomiated as the best indie developer and many more!
